Copa America 2021:

Tournament Format

The 10 South American member nations started off with a group stage from which eight teams advanced to a single-elimination knockout phase that started at the quarterfinal stage.

Knockout Stage:

The single-elimination knockouts began with the quarterfinals and ran all the way to the final. The four teams qualifying from Group A faced the four teams advancing from Group B with respective top seeds facing the lowest seeds in the opposite group: 1A vs. 4B, 1B vs. 4A, 2A vs. 3B, 2B vs. 3A.

Group Stage:

The 10 teams were split up into two groups of five. After round-robin play, the top four finishers in each group advanced to the quarterfinals.
